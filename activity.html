TOKYO

(Capital of Japan)

Tokyo, Japan's busy capital,mixes the ultramodern and the traditional,from neon-lit skyscapers to historic temples. The opulent Meiji Shinto Shrine is known for its towering gate and sorrounding woods. The Imperial palace sits amid large public gardens. The city's many museums offer exhibits ranging from classical art (in the Tokyo National Museum) to a reconstructed kabuki theater (in the Edo-Tokyo Museum).
